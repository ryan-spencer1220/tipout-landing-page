Tip Out

Mobile Tip Tracker

Tip Out is a mobile application designed to help service industry workers track their income from tips. Log cash tips, credit tips, and income from wages to see up to date data viualizations. Users can store their settings, toggle between light/dark mode, and view income based analytics!
